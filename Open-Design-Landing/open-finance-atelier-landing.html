II. The premise.Owned finance records 002 / 008
Manifesto / civic ledger

Finance records should feel owned, like a civic register.

Open Finance is for people who want the clarity of a finance dashboard without handing every transaction to another cloud-first app. The product keeps the workspace local by default, then lets the user decide when to connect Google Sheets or Cloudflare backup.

Local-first posture

The money file starts on device, with user-controlled exports and an explicit sync boundary.

Spreadsheet escape

Budget tables still exist where they help, but the interface handles context, status, and review.
